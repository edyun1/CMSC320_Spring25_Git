Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Great tool for managing your coding projects. Allows for great collaboration with others and the
ability to manage your work/versions of projects.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to business analysis. For example, a business analyst could use
data science to visualize or even predict the status of the company's revenue/financial status with many
inputs that could be updated.
